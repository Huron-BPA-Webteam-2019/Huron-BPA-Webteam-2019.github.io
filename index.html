What is a Bio-home?

BioHome

/bioHome/

Noun

A bio-home (or eco-home) is an environmentally low-impact home designed and built using materials and technology that reduces its carbon footprint and lowers its energy needs.

Biophilic Homes

Biophilic homes use the surrounding environment as a design inspiration, and create a closer connection to nature and the environment.

Read More
Neutral Homes

Neutral homes leave no carbon footprint behind. They significantly decrease the heating needs required in a regular home, and are also cost-friendly.

Read More
Prefabricated Homes

A prefabricated home is built in a factory and shipped to the location desired. They are cost-effective and minimize waste compared to regular homes.

Read More
Earthship Homes

Earthship homes are made from self-recycled or natural material, and are self-sustaining. They are energy efficient and cost effective.
