Biophillic Homes



Biophillic homes are one type of biohome.
Some types of biophillic houses include natural light houses, living wall houses, and green roof houses.
These all help to incorporate nature in these houses.

Biophillic homes are a product of biophillic design, which is the process of incorporating natural elements into the build.
It tries to focus on the sensory elements of humans, such as visual, auditory, and olfactory senses.
The design is modified to help mimic natural environments that appeal to these senses.
This design helps to connect people to nature

Earthship Homes

These Earthship bio homes are able to use less energy and are more cost-friendly, which is very helpful and nice.
They are self sustaining systems, meaning that they can last for a long time; they are durable
They are made from self-recycled or natural material, which is beneficial to the environment
Energy is derived from solar or wind power with small scale windmills and solar panels.

Neutral Homes

Leaves no carbon footprint behind.

Prefab Houses

Prefab houses utilize measurements which are exact to ensure that there is no waste left over in the process.
They are pre-made houses
They are constructed with eco-friendly solar panels
They can also come in various luxury designs
They are also very cost-effective price range of $10,000 to $15,000)
