Types: Biophillic Earthship Neutral Prefab

Biophillic Homes

Biophillic homes are one type of biohome, meaning
These biophillic homes are able to use less energy and are more cost-friendly, which is very helpful and nice.

Some types of biophillic houses include natural light houses, living wall houses, and green roof houses.
These all help to incorporate nature in these houses.

Earthship Homes

Self sustaining systems
Made from self-recycled or natural material
Energy is derived from solar or wind power with small scale windmills and solar panels.
Construction methods were applied to the construction of the Great Wall Of China.
Construction makes it very resistant to weathering.
Walls range from 19-24 inches which makes it extremely quiet and quickly adapts to changes in noise levels.

Neutral Homes

Leaves no carbon footprint behind.
Contain solar panels, insulation, and water pumps.
Significantly decreases heating needs.
Cost-friendly.

Prefab Houses

Prefab houses utilize measurements which are exact to ensure that there is no waste left over in the process.
They are pre-made houses
They are constructed with eco-friendly solar panels
They can also come in various luxury designs
They are also very cost-effective price range of $10,000 to $15,000)
